Jumpstart 2019

360DigiTMG is thrilled announce the second edition of our very popular scholarship program – Jumpstart. Last year the program was offered under the banner of ExcelR. This year, our name has changed and our offering has been enhanced with up to 90% fee discounts! Our specially tailored courses have been created to offer 360 degrees of opportunities to students and working professionals. 

Programs offered

  • Data Analytics for Auditors
  • AI Based Conversational Chat Bot Building
  • Machine Learning on Cloud
  • Big Data for Managers
  • Exclusive Python and R programming for Beginners

About Scholarship

The scholarship is being issued by 360DigiTMG in association with Innodatatics Inc., USA. It will be an annual scholarship program and will be open to students across Malaysia.

Objectives

  • To ensure that students learn valuable skills to launch or advance their careers in an AI and data-driven economy.
  • The course curriculum has been designed keeping in mind trending and future skill requirements
  • Giving students the opportunity to upskill themselves for the thousands of AI and data-driven jobs available in leading organizations across the globe

About the Sponsor

Rated among the "20 Most Promising Data Analytics Solution Provider of 2018", Innodatatics uses research, data quality assessment and measurement system analysis, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis and business reporting to arrive at customised and innovative solutions for clients. Innodatatics also offers a range of products such as Chatbot and Server Logo Analytics to meet specific client requirements. The organization is keen to support and encourage upcoming data professionals and they believe that the need for skilled data scientists in only going to grow in the coming years.

How it Works

This scholarship is open to students across Malaysia who have completed their graduation/under graduation/diploma. The scholarships will be offered on a first come first serve basis – so the sooner you apply the greater your chances of availing the benefits! We will have a total of 100 scholarships (two batches of 50 students each). Students who receive the scholarship will pay only for the certification and program deliverables. Which means that they can enjoy the course at a huge discount!

Duration: - 16 hours

Key Modules

• Understand the advantages of Big Data Analytics on both structured & unstructured data
• Understand the stages of Big Data Analytics and Data flow in the technology stack 

AI BASED CONVERSATIONAL CHAT BOT BUILDING

Duration: - 16 hours

KEY MODULES
• NLP Implementations for Chatbot Development
